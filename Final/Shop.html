Online Retailers

  • Amazon offers a wide selection of perfumes from various brands. You can read customer reviews and choose from a range of options.
  • Sephora is a popular beauty retailer that carries a diverse range of perfumes, including both mainstream and niche brands.
  • Nordstrom is a department store that offers a variety of high-end and designer fragrances.

Specialty Perfume Stores

  1. Perfume Boutiques: Many cities have specialty perfume boutiques that focus on unique and niche fragrances. These boutiques often provide a more personalized shopping experience.
  2. Department Stores: Major department stores like Macy's, Bloomingdale's, and Saks Fifth Avenue typically have a dedicated fragrance section with a wide range of options.
