Perfumes!

I am in love with the smell of some of the new perfumes out there. Perfumes may not be so cheap but you can scent them without having to spend a lot of money! You can just walk into any perfume brand store and try out their scent. Some brands like Sephora, Dior, and L’Oreal, as well as niche perfume companies like Nordstorm, are even kind enough to give out free samples for you to spray on yourself. I love to try out new perfumes whenever I go to the mall and sometimes I even end up buying them to add to my perfume collection!
