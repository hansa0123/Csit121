Udon Noodles

This recipie is super simple and delicous I learnt it from my cousin

Ingredients needed

  • Udon Noodle pack
  • One small onion
  • Baby boc choy
  • Mushrooms
  • Sugar
  • Sesame oil
  • Soy Sauce
  • Chili oil
  • Water
  • Oil(any)

Preparing The Recipe

  1. Bring the water to boil and put thr noodles to boil
  2. In a seprate pot put oil and your chopped onions and lett that cook
  3. after your onion brown add in your mushroom let it cook snd then add baby boc choy
  4. after your noodles are cooked add then into thr pot with your mushrooms and baby boc choy
  5. add your seasonings sugar, soy sauce, sasame oil, and chili oil
  6. Thats it your ready to eat and you can add more seasonings if you would like to

This is my favorite recipie because it is simple and easy to make and it taste delicious
