Udon Noodles

This recipe Is created by Hansa. Traditional Japanese Recipe

Ingredients

Serves 2 people

  • Onion
  • Sesame Oil
  • Soy Sauce
  • Chili Oil
  • Sugar
  • Noodles
  • Baby Bok Choy
  • Mushrooms
  • Water
  • Oil

Preparing the Recipe

  1. Bring the water to a boil and put your noodles in and let them cook.
  2. In a separate pot, put oil and your chopped onions, let that simmer, then add your mushrooms, let those cook a little and add your baby bok choy.
  3. After your noodles are done, add them into the pot with mushrooms and baby bok choy.
  4. Add your seasonings: sugar, soy sauce, chili oil, and sesame oil.
  5. Adjust seasonings.
  6. Serve.
