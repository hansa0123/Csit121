Udon Noodles

This receipe Is created by Hansa. Traditional Japanese Recipe

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Seseame Oil
  • Soy Sauce
  • chili oil
  • sugar
  • Noodles
  • baby boc choy
  • mushrooms
  • water
  • oil

Preparing the Recipe

  1. bring the water to a boil and put your noodles in and let them cook
  2. In a separte pot put oil and your chopped onions let that simmer then add your mushrooms let those cook a little and add your baby bok choy. .
  3. after your noodles are done add them into the pot with mushrooms and baby bok choy.
  4. add you seasosings sugar, soy sauce, chili oil and seasme oil.
  5. adjust seasonings
  6. serve
